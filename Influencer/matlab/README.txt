Functions:
1. plot_activity:  plot the proportion of active followers of each influencer group by KI and KO respectively.
2. plot_activity_gender: plot the proportion of follower's gender of each influencer group by KI and KO respectively.
3. plot_ki_fans_k: plot the number of friends and followers of influencers' fans V.S. KI
4. plot_ko_fans_k: plot the number of friends and followers of influencers' fans V.S. KO
5. plot_k_fans_statuses: plot the number of posts of fans' group by influencers' KI and KO.
6. plot_urank_dist: plot the distribution of fans' urank group by influencers' KI and KO.
7. plot_vt_activity: plot the proportion of active followers of each influencer group by verified type.
